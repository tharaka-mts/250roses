Frequently Asked

Client Etiquette & Questions.

Everything you need to know before making your first inquiry.

How do I make a booking with 250 Roses?
+

Bookings begin with a discreet inquiry through any of our secure channels — email, WhatsApp, Telegram, or the form above. Once received, our concierge team will guide you through the selection and scheduling process.

Note: We do not accept walk-in bookings. All arrangements are made in advance to ensure the highest quality experience for both client and Rose.

What information do I need to provide?
+

To ensure the safety and comfort of our talent, all clients are required to complete a brief verification process. This typically includes a form of identification, and may include a social media profile or professional reference for first-time clients.

All information is handled with absolute discretion and deleted from our systems within 30 days of engagement.

How far in advance should I book?
+

We recommend booking at least 48–72 hours in advance for standard arrangements. For events, travel, or multi-day engagements, a minimum of 1 week notice is preferred to ensure the ideal match is available.

Last-minute requests are accommodated subject to availability. Premium rates may apply for same-day or next-day bookings.

What is the expected etiquette with a Rose?
+

Our Roses are elite professionals deserving of the highest respect. We expect all clients to conduct themselves with dignity and courtesy at all times. Inappropriate behaviour will result in immediate termination of the engagement and permanent removal from our client list.

Treat every interaction as you would with a distinguished colleague: with grace, discretion, and respect for boundaries.

Are communications between clients and Roses private?
+

Yes. All communications are handled through encrypted channels and are never logged, stored, or disclosed. We operate under a strict Zero Data Retention policy for all client interactions.

Direct contact details of our Roses are never shared until both parties have consented to an engagement.

Do you offer travel and international arrangements?
+

Yes. Many of our Roses are available for domestic and international travel. Travel, accommodation, and all associated costs are arranged separately and transparently. Please indicate your destination and dates in your inquiry.

We operate in 12 cities globally including London, Paris, Dubai, Milan, Monaco, Ibiza, New York, and more.

What payment methods are accepted?
+

We accept bank transfers, cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Monero), and select digital payment platforms. All transactions are processed securely and discretely.

A deposit is required to confirm all bookings. Full balance is due 24 hours prior to engagement. Detailed payment instructions are provided upon booking confirmation.

What is the cancellation policy?
+

Cancellations made 48+ hours in advance receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit the deposit. Same-day cancellations may be charged at the full rate.

We understand that circumstances change — our concierge team will always work with you to find a suitable alternative arrangement whenever possible.
